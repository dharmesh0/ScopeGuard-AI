# Roadmap

## v2

- Signed plugin manifests and plugin marketplace support
- Fine-grained policy engine for approvals and scope templates
- Richer GraphQL schema and subscriptions
- Evidence diffing across recurring assessments
- Webhooks and Slack / Teams notifications

## v3

- Kubernetes-native runner pool with Jobs or isolated executor pods
- Multi-tenant organization model
- SSO, SCIM, and audit export integrations
- Advanced graph analytics for attack-surface clustering
- Policy-as-code and compliance profile packs

## Longer-term ideas

- SBOM ingestion and dependency exposure correlation
- Artifact retention policies and immutable reporting
- Enterprise secrets brokers and delegated short-lived credentials
- Custom evaluation harnesses for LLM-backed security triage quality
